Five formulas

  • Bayes: P(H|E) = P(E|H)P(H)/P(E) — denominator via LOTP.
  • Linearity: E[ΣXᵢ] = ΣE[Xᵢ], dependence be damned.
  • Variance: Var(X) = E[X²] − (E[X])²; independent ⇒ variances add.
  • Inclusion–exclusion: |A∪B| = |A|+|B|−|A∩B| (alternate signs beyond).
  • First-step: E_state = 1 + Σ p·E_next, absorbing states = 0.

Night-before drills

  1. Derive HH = 6 from the two state equations, out loud, under 2 minutes.
  2. Disease test at 95% accuracy, 1/500 prevalence — do the 1000-person head count.
  3. Ruin: ₹10 vs ₹90, fair — win probability and expected length. (0.1; 900.)
  4. State the ants-pass-through trick and the coin-flip-group trick from memory — both are pure invariants.
  5. Say the Monty Hall "host knows" caveat unprompted.
